How to Play

- There are 3 levels. The last level is the boss level.
- Use the arrows or W,A,S,D to move your character.
- Press the spacebar to jump.
- Avoid tricky obstacles along the way.
- Complete the level before the timer ends (60secs).
- Collect the candies that was spilt.
- Defeat Bunny the Bandit in the final battle!
