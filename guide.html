Game Guide

Control the Sprite

Use the Left and Right arrow keys to move the sprite. Press Spacebar to make the sprite jump.

Collect the Candy

Walk towards the candy to collect each one. Keep an eye out for all the candies as you go!

Avoid the Red Spikes

Be careful of the red spikes! They appear along the way and must be avoided to stay safe.

Timer and Lives

You have 5 lives. It will decrease when you stepped on the spike. Be careful!
There is a timer in level 1 & 2. Complete the level before the timer ends.

The Final Task

At the end of the game, you have to shoot the bunny with the candy. Aim carefully to ensure it lands correctly to complete the game. Click anywhere to shoot.
