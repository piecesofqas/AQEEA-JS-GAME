Game Storyline

Ellie is instructed by her mother to deliver a basket of sweets to her sick grandmother, her grandmother's house in the Peppermint Forest area. Unfortunately, during the journey, a rabbit known for his mischievous nature, Bandit the Bunny steals the basket of sweets, what will happen next? stay tuned.
